## Research Notes

### Accessing Hawaiian Knowledge

**Proper Protocol**:
1. Seek permission from Hawaiian cultural authorities
2. Approach with respect for sacred knowledge
3. Understand some knowledge may not be shared publicly
4. Center Hawaiian voices and perspectives
5. Support Hawaiian cultural sovereignty

### Knowledge Restrictions

Some knowledge about Huaka'i pō is:
- Sacred and not meant for public documentation
- Protected within Hawaiian families and communities
- Taught only through proper cultural channels
- Subject to kapu (sacred restrictions)

**Researchers should**:
- Respect these boundaries
- Consult with Hawaiian cultural practitioners
- Understand limitations of written sources
- Not pressure for information that should remain protected
